# Vanilla Almond Milk

## Ingredients

| :--- | ---: | :--- |
| Raw, unsaltened almonds | 100-200 | g |
| Vanilla pod | 1 |  |
| (Filtered) water | 1 | l |
| Salt | 0.5 | TSP |

## Directions

1. Soften almonds with salt 8-12 hours in water.
2. Mix almonds with water and vanilla pod.
3. Strain through nut milk bag.

Shelf life: 3-4 days in refrigerator.
